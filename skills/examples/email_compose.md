# Email Composition Skill

**Keywords:** email, send, compose, write, mail, message
**Triggers:** "send email", "compose email", "write to", "email someone"

## Description
Composes professional emails using best practices and proper formatting.

## Instructions for AI

When composing an email:

1. **Identify recipient** and purpose
2. **Determine tone:** formal, casual, or professional
3. **Structure properly:**
   - Subject line (clear, concise)
   - Greeting
   - Body (2-4 paragraphs)
   - Closing
   - Signature

4. **Ask for approval** before sending
5. **Use Proton Bridge** to send via encrypted email

## Parameters
- `to`: Recipient email (required)
- `subject`: Email subject (required)
- `tone`: formal/casual/professional (default: professional)
- `body`: Email content or key points

## Best Practices
- Keep subject under 50 characters
- Use proper salutation based on relationship
- Be concise - aim for under 200 words
- Include clear call-to-action if needed
- Proofread for typos
